Music taste

I'm a pure rock listener — whatever the subgenre is, I’ll try ^^. Here are some of my favourite tracks (I can't name them all):

  • Pink Floyd — Any Colour You Like, Comfortably Numb, Hey You
  • Oasis — Half the World Away, Don’t Look Back in Anger
  • David Bowie — Heroes, Station to Station
  • My Chemical Romance — Thank You for the Venom, Demolition Lovers
  • The Beatles — I Want You (She's So Heavy), Here, There and Everywhere
  • Green Day — Jesus of Suburbia, Stray Heart

I am all about the mids; I don’t like piercing treble or too much bass that wrecks my ears :/
